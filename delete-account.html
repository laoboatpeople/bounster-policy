Request Account & Data Deletion

Submit a deletion request for your Bounster account. We will erase your profile and associated data (subject to legal retention obligations) within 30 days and notify you at the email you provide.

Quick email request

This opens your email app with a prefilled message.

Email support@bounster.app to request deletion

What happens next

  • We verify the request via your account email.
  • We schedule deletion of your account and associated content (e.g., profile, photos, activity posts).
  • We send a confirmation once deletion is complete.

Alternative: in-app deletion

If you’re signed in, you can also delete your account directly in the app: Profile → Settings → Delete Account.
